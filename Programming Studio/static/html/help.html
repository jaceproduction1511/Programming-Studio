Welcome, Adventurer!

The world you are about to explore contains mystery, danger, and rich rewards for those who can successfully navigate it's labyrinth. This page will serve as your guide to avoid the multitude of perils that await you.

You are about to enter the lair of the WumpusTM. This foul smelling beast has hidden a great treasure of shiny, klinky, gold somewhere in the cave. You are tasked with the objective of delving into this dangerous Lair, collecting the gold, and reaching the Exit Door with your prize. Alive

This is you:


At the beginning of the match you will be placed at a random location in the Wumpus's lair. The Lair consists of many tiles, or rooms arranged into a rectangular grid.

The map:


At most locations on the map, you may move to the position above, below, left, or right of you as long as that move wouldn't take you off of the map.

Additionally, there is a "Fog of War" covering the map, meaning that (in most cases) a tile/room must be visited before its contents are revealed and displayed on the map.

The environment possesses several important features that you should be aware of:

  • Pits:
    Some rooms have had great sinkholes open up underneath them. Entering a room containing one of these pits causes you to slip and fall down to your death.
    You will feel a Cool Breeze () if you are in a tile/room that is adjacent to a pit.
  • Wumpus:
    A horrible smelly monster that occupies this Lair. The Wumpus is absolutely lethal and will immediately eat anyone unfortunate enough to occupy the same room.
    You will smell a Horrible Stench () if you are in a tile/room that is adjacent to a Wumpus.
    • The Wumpus can be killed using an Arrow (Arrow usage is described later in this help screen)
    • Additionally, when a Wumpus is killed, all Players will hear a blood curdling noise resonate throughout the Chamber
  • Gold:
    The great treasure, hoarded by the Wumpus. It is hidden away in one of the rooms somewhere in the Lair.
    You will see the Glitter of Gold () if you are in the same tile/room with the gold.


Movement in this game works in a turn based manner. You can select a direction to move in the next turn by clicking on the arrows next to your player character. When both players have selected a move, the turn will execute.


There are numerous items that can be found on the map and each is used for a different purpose. Items are placed around the map randomly at the beginning of the game and, additionally, may be spawned via random events in all difficulty levels.


Gold: This is Gold. The prize! Your Gold will be dropped on the ground in a random loaction if you die while possessing it.


Life Potion:This rare item will grant you an extra life! It is definitely your lucky day if you find one!


Ability Potion:This item grants an extra use of your ability (this only applies to the Scout, adding an extra usage of his reveal ability.)


Arrow: An Arrow! (Arrows are explained more fully in the following section)

Items can be picked up by clicking on the square you are currently occupying. you will then pick up everything in that tile.


Most class types are given 1 arrow at the beginning of the match. (With the notable exception of the Hunter class, who is given 2)

Using an arrow works similarly to movement. A notable difference being that arrows are fired instantaneously rather than after the turn executes.

Arrows will travel until they kill something or encounter the edge of the map. If an arrow encouters the edge of the map It will drop onto the ground at that location and can be picked up like any other item.


There are two game modes to choose from:

  • Competitive/Player vs Player:
    • In this game mode, players compete against each other to be the first to retrieve the gold and return it to the Exit Door or defeat eachother in ranged combat.
    • There is a "Fog of War" present on the map, and each Player can only see the tiles/rooms they have previously visited.
    • Players my use arrows to kill other players.
  • Collaborative/Co-Op:
    • In this game mode, players work together to retrieve the gold and return it to the Exit Door. If one Player completes the goal, the entire Team wins. If all players lose all of their lives, the Team loses.
    • The "Fog of War" is slightly different in this mode. All revealed tiles revealed by any player, anywhere on the map, are made visible to all other players on the Team.


There are three Difficulty Levels to choose from:

  • Wuss: Easy Mode.
    • In this difficulty, "what you see is what you get."
    • Wumpuses do not move
    • positive Random events will occur occasionally, including random drops of arrows and potions
  • Explorer: Medium Mode.
    • The Wumpuses will move occasionally.
    • Random Events will happen occasionally (both beneficial and detrimental)
  • Vlad the Impaler (Vlad Tepes): TryHard Mode.
    • The Wumpuses will more often.
    • Random Events will happen more often (both beneficial and detrimental, but weighted more towards the negative side.)


There are four Character Classes to choose from:

Character selection occurs after joining or hosting a game. Additionally, the game will not begin until both players have selected a class.

  • Scout:
    • This character can reveal any tile on the map.
    • The Scout character will start with 5 usages of thier ability, which can be actived by clicking on "scout" under "abilities" and then selecting a map tile to reveal. These may be replenished by finding ability potions on the map and picking them up.
  • Hunter:
    • The Hunter will get one extra Arrow at the beginning of the match. Go nuts Elmer Fudd.
  • Prospector:
    • The Prospector can sense the gold from one tile away. Capitalism.
  • Survivor:
    • The Survivor will get one extra Life at the beginning of the match.
